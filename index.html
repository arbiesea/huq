Introduction

Thank you for contributing data to the H-Unique research. On this page we will guide you through the submission of photographs of your hands and the collection of supporting information.

Information is grouped in four sections which can be accessed by clicking on the section headings below. The minimum information we require for the project is three images per hand. Providing additional information is optional but doing so will aid significantly in our research.

Explanatory notes are given at the top of each section and further informtion can be viewed (or hidden) by clicking the icon wherever it is displayed.

At the end of the form, before any data is transferred you will also be required to indicate your consent to particate in the project. Once your data is uploaded you will be given a unique reference code which you should make a note of, as all information is collected anonymously, you will need this code if you change your mind in the future and wish to withdraw your data from the research.

Section 1: Demographic Info

Demographic information tells us a little about your general characteristics and is used to help classify the hand photos that you submit. All of the information in this section is optional but providing it will help us to tune our models more accurately.
